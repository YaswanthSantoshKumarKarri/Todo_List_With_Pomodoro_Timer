The core process of the pomodoros Technique consists of 6 steps:

1. Choose a task you'd like to get done

2. Set the pomodoros for 25 minutes

3. Work on the task until the pomodoros timer is up

4. When your task is done, click on the task to strike off

5. Take a short break of 5 minutes and continue with the next pomodoros

6. for Every 4 pomodoros, take a longer break
